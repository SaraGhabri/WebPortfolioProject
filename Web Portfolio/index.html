SPONSORINI

Sponsorini Way, executed in 2021, is a dynamic MERN Stack platform bridging the gap between business owners and event organizers. It serves as a central hub for businesses seeking strategic partnerships and sponsorships. Offering specialized marketing counseling courses, it empowers businesses with the knowledge to refine their marketing strategies. Additionally, the platform provides dedicated spaces for small businesses, amplifying their visibility. Sponsorini Way redefines collaboration, making it a valuable destination for unlocking opportunities, fostering partnerships, and navigating the competitive business landscape.

ODM

OD Model, also known as Orange Development Model is mainly a project related to data savyness, analysis and interpretation. It is a project established in Orange Franchise using Google data studio, google spreadsheets and Power BI in order to visualize all the data regarding clients, the franchise's incomes, most performing agent etc.. It simply summarizes all aspects of a business from finance to HR to operations through counting certain KPIs in order to be able to exctract strategies from it.
